## Context
Positioning, primary persona (operator-builder), and primary action ("Start a Tree readiness
check") are set. Now design the **sitemap and information architecture** for v2 — and crucially,
how the cinematic **living globe coexists with a fast, functional site** without making the
homepage feel like a setup wizard.

## Scope — IN
- **Sitemap** — top-level sections/routes and navigation.
- **Globe ↔ site relationship** — is the globe the hero, a route (/atlas), or both? How does a
  visitor get from wonder → the primary action → deeper info.
- **Paths for the 3 journeys** (operator-builder, data evaluator, curious explorer) through the IA.
- **Where the primary action lives** and how it stays prominent without dominating.
- **Progressive disclosure rules** — what's shown first vs. revealed on demand (respect performance + SEO).

## Scope — OUT (hand back)
- Visual/layout design and pixels (Lead). - Copywriting (Grok). - Rendering tech / globe implementation (Lead).
