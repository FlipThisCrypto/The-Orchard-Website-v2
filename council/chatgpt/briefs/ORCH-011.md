## Context
Positioning is provisionally set ("community-owned environmental data infrastructure"). Now we
decide **who we serve first** and **the one thing** a first-time visitor should do. This drives the
information architecture (next task, ORCH-012) and what the globe must funnel toward.

## Scope — IN
- **Rank the audiences** (crypto-native operators now → researchers/cities/farmers/insurers/
  educators later), with reasoning. Name the **one primary persona** for launch.
- Define the **single most important action** a first-time visitor should take within 60 seconds,
  and why everything should support it.
- Map **2–3 key journeys** (e.g., operator, data-buyer, curious explorer) at a high level.

## Scope — OUT (hand back)
