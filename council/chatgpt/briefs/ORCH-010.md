## Context
This is the keystone. The single sentence that says what The Orchard IS in 2036 drives the
information architecture, the messaging, and every page of v2. It must be true to the anti-hype,
hardware-first, open-source ethos and still make sense to an enterprise/research buyer a decade
out — not a 6-month token narrative.

## Scope — IN
- One **recommended** one-sentence positioning for The Orchard in 2036 (what it is, for whom, why
  it matters now and in 10 years), with a short rationale.
- The **category** we intend to own, and why.
- 2–3 **alternate** positioning sentences, each with trade-offs.
- Lead with the recommendation, then the alternates.
