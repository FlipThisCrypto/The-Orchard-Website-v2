## Context
Who actually pays for environmental sensor data, and for what? This feeds the financial model
(ORCH-022) and the data-buyer framing in the IA and messaging. Rigor and citations over breadth.

## Scope — IN
- **Buyer segments** for environmental/hyperlocal sensor data (academic, municipal, insurance,
  agtech, climate/ESG, weather) — who, why, and how they procure. Cite sources.
- **Rank use cases by fit** for The Orchard specifically (air quality, temp/humidity, pressure,
  particulates, etc.), with reasoning.
- **Price & channel notes** where you can source them (typical deal shapes, API vs. report vs.
  feed). Label anything unsourced as an estimate with method.

## Scope — OUT (hand back)
- Sales/marketing copy (Grok). - IA/UX (ChatGPT). - The full financial model (ORCH-022).
