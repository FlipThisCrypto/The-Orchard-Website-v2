## How this folder works
- **`briefs/`** — your assignments. The Lead drops a Task Brief here (e.g. `ORCH-020.md`) as we go.
  Read the latest one, follow it exactly, and return a Deliverable in the
  [Deliverable template](../../governance/templates/deliverable.md) format.
- **`submissions/`** — your returned work, filed here by the Lead (raw, versioned: `ORCH-###-vN.md`)
  before review and integration into `docs/research/`. You don't write here — the Lead does.

## The rules (always)
One task at a time, your lane only. **Cite every number, claim, and quote** (label estimates as
estimates and show the method). Separate fact from estimate from assumption. **Never fabricate
data, studies, or links.** No scope creep. Out-of-scope ideas → a "Proposed follow-ups" list.
